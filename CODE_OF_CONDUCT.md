# Code of Conduct — Nova_A

## Our Pledge
We commit to maintaining a welcoming, harassment-free community for all contributors.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that promotes positive environment:
- Constructive feedback  
- Respectful language  
- Helping newcomers  

Unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment  
- Personal attacks  
- Political or sensitive topics  
- Publishing private information  

## Enforcement
Violations may result in:
- Warnings  
- Temporary ban  
- Permanent ban  

Project maintainers’ decisions are final.

Thank you for keeping Nova_A a friendly community!
